Frequently Asked Questions

Your adoption team handles all the marketing and coordination. We need your input for key decisions and preferences, which is typically 1 - 2 hours per month, unless you are communicating with an expectant mom. Think of us as your adoption management team, letting you focus on your preparation for parenthood.

Our timeline is based on decades of experience and thousands of successful adoptions. We coordinate the details of your process and only refer the best providers, eliminating common delays and bottlenecks that can slow down the process. We are active across the nation, significantly increasing opportunities for placement.

Your small, personal adoption team manages your adoption journey, and will be there for anything you need. Plus, our 100+ professional network means no time wasted finding and vetting providers. Our company was started by two adoptive mothers, who wanted to bring their experience and shared adoption knowledge to others. Our dedicated team, decades of experience, and ethical approach makes us stand out.

Within 24 hours, you'll hear from our admissions team who will help answer your adoption questions and help set up your private consultation. No waiting periods - we begin working for you right away.

We have a process that has earned the trust of expectant moms for over 25 years. We will only present you with opportunities that match your preferences. While every situation has some level of risk, the team will work together with the expectant mom to verify medical records and commitment levels.

Approximately 90% of our families successfully adopt within 24 months. This is driven by three factors: our extensive nationwide network, rigorous screening processes, and experienced directors who know how to navigate complex situations. We are active in all states, working to give you the most exposure possible.

 Every step has built-in safeguards through our legal team, compliance experts, and proven processes. We've refined our risk management approach over 25 years. Your personal adoption coordinator anticipates and prevents common issues before they arise.

Challenges may be a part of the process. Rest assured, our team knows how to navigate any situation. Our network includes specialized experts for every type of concern. Plus, we have backup plans and alternatives ready - you're never without options.

Think of us as your personal concierge service, guiding you along the way. If an expectant mom chooses to place with you, there will be a need for you to hire additional service providers, but we will give you those referrals. You won’t have to worry about finding them or doing the research yourself.
