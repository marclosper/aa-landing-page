Frequently Asked Questions

How much time will this require of me?

Your adoption team handles all the marketing and coordination. We need your input for key decisions and preferences, which is typically 1 - 2 hours per month, unless you are communicating with an expectant mom. Think of us as your adoption management team, letting you focus on your preparation for parenthood.

What makes your timeline reliable?

Our 27 years of experience and proven process ensure consistent timelines. We maintain relationships with birth mothers nationwide and have streamlined procedures that eliminate common delays.

How are you different from other adoption professionals?

We provide comprehensive support including emotional counseling, legal guidance, and post-adoption resources. Our ethical approach and transparent process set us apart in the industry.

What happens after I make the initial call?

We'll schedule a consultation to understand your needs, explain our process, and answer all your questions. If we're a good fit, we'll begin your adoption journey with a personalized plan.

How do you screen potential birth mothers?

We have a thorough screening process that includes medical, legal, and counseling evaluations to ensure the best outcomes for all parties involved.

What's your success rate and what drives it?

Our 92% success rate within 24 months is driven by our experienced team, ethical practices, comprehensive support system, and strong relationships with birth mothers nationwide.

How do you protect my interests throughout the process?

We provide independent legal counsel, transparent communication, and comprehensive contracts that protect your interests while ensuring ethical treatment of all parties.

What happens if there are complications?

Our experienced team handles complications with professionalism and care. We provide support, guidance, and alternative solutions to keep your adoption journey on track.

Will I have to deal with multiple agencies or providers?

No, we coordinate with all necessary professionals on your behalf. You'll have one primary contact who manages your entire adoption journey from start to finish.
